## What is Mina? Mina是什么?

Mina is the first cryptocurrency protocol with a succinct blockchain. Current cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum store hundreds of gigabytes of data, and as time goes on, their blockchains will only increase in size. With Mina however, no matter how much the usage grows, the blockchain always stays the same size - about 22kb<Footnote refNumber="1" link="/blog/22kb-sized-blockchain-a-technical-reference" /> (the size of a few tweets). This means participants can quickly sync and verify the network.

Mina 是第一个具有简洁区块链的加密货币协议。当前的比特币和以太坊等加密货币存储了数百GB的数据，随着时间的推移，它们的区块链只会越来越大。然而，对于 Mina，无论使用量增长多少，区块链始终保持相同的大小 - 大约 22kb<Footnote refNumber="1" link="/blog/22kb-sized-blockchain-a-technical-reference" /> ( 几条推文的大小）。 这意味着参与者可以快速同步和验证网络。

This breakthrough is made possible due to zk-SNARKs - a type of succinct cryptographic proof. Each time a Mina node produces a new block, it also generates a SNARK proof verifying that the block was valid. All nodes can then store the small proof, as opposed to the entire chain. By not having to worry about block size, the Mina protocol enables a blockchain that is decentralized at scale.

这一突破成为可能是由于 zk-SNARKs（零知识证明） —— 一种简洁的密码证明。 每次 Mina 节点生成一个新块时，它还会生成一个 SNARK 证明来验证该块是有效的。 然后所有节点都可以存储一种足够小的证明，而不是整个链。由于不必担心区块大小，Mina 协议支持大规模分散的区块链。
